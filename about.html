ABOUT
MYSELF

Hey there! Harry Tones at your service. I'm a product designer and front-end developer based in Lagos, Nigeria. Designing has always been a fascination of mine, especially when it comes to the finer details of a product. That's why I started my career as a designer three years ago. Since then, I've been designing mobile apps, websites, and utilizing design systems to create the perfect user experience.

But my passion for design didn't stop there. I also wanted to learn how to build my designs from scratch, so I learned front-end development languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Now I can bring my designs to life and create stunning animations. This is actually a super power most designers wish they had.

When I'm not designing or writing code, you can find me swimming laps of freestyle in the pool, playing FIFA with a friend, or watching an interesting movie. I believe that a balance of work and play is essential for a creative mind like mine.
